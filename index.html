Hi! I'm Samantha!

I am completing my masters in Human Computer Interaction at DePaul University. After finishing my Bachelors of Science in Psychology at University of California, Davis, I began working as a teacher for autistic students at a charity organization. Learning how to teach students who require different methods to process information, I began to be interested in user experience.

This past year, the courses that I have taken have taught me skills in identifying target users and creating scenarios and storyboards to help facilitate development of a product geared towards users. I have also developed skills in creating wireframes and prototypes, and using iterative development to improve on those prototypes by testing it against users.

I am looking for the opportunity to implement and improve on my skills in any opportunity that I receive.
